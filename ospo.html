Microsoft's Open Source Program

Enterprise-scale open source

Microsoft has a strong open source program that encourages contribution, allows engineers to use open source, and easily release projects.

We are committed to helping share what we have learned in adopting open source at the Enterprise Scale by sharing tools, services, guidance, and our learnings.

The Microsoft open source program is a part of the One Engineering System (1ES) organization at the company and is supported by the Open Source Standards and Legal Team, an Open Source Executive Council, and Open Source Champs and others from around the world.

The open source program at Microsoft is OpenChain 2.0 conformant.
